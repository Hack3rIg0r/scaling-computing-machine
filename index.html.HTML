General controls

  • Arrows: arrows are used to move
  • W: change weopon
  • E: use ability
  • Mouse pressed: shoot(you can hold)

Shots

  • Regular: 10 shots/s
  • Bomb: when an enemy is hit, -1hp for all enemies in a radius of 50, if it hits big cube -10hp, 1 shot/s
  • Pierce: shot goes through multiple enemies, 4 shots/s
  • scather: shoot 20 bullets in a spread, 1 shot/s

Abilities

  • Freeze: all enemies stop for 5s every 15s
  • 3x: triples points for 10s every 20s
  • pound: kill all enemies in a radius of 100 every 15s

Enemy info

  • normal enemies: green:3 HP yellow: 2 HP red:1 HP
  • blue enemies: shoots at you
  • big cube: shoots at you, slower, 50HP gets darker when it has less HP
